- o: on/off
- r: toggle recording
- f: toggle floodlight
- spacebar: toggle DLP

- j: decrement origin in x
- l: increment origin in x
- k: decerment origin in y
- i: increment origin in y
- a: decrement radius in x
- d: increment radius in x
- w: increment radius in y
- s: decrement radius in y

- F: flip head tail ( only persists if temporal head/tail intellegence is also enabled)
- t: toggle temporal head/tail inteligence

- p: toggle protocol
- ,: decrement protocol step
- .: increment protocol step

- [: decrement threshold
- ]: increment threshold

- v: invert selection 
- V: reflect illumination pattern over centerline (flip dorsal ventral)

- /: timed pulse illumination
- <: decrement duration of pulsed illumination
- >: increment duration of pulsed illumination

- <tab>: Turn stage tracker on/off
- X: increment stage velocity
- Z: decrement stage velocity 
- <del>: emergency stage shutoff

- G: increment Gaussian Radius size
- g: decrement Gaussian Radius size

- u: start/stop a head-to-tail illumination sweep
- U: toggle direction of head-to-tail illumination sweep

- q: toggle timed secondary protocol step
